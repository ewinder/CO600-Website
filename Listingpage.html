Occupying a substantial plot of approximately one acre with sweeping views across the Kent countryside and The Swale, this purpose-built Oast House-designed home with numerous outbuildings offers versatile living accommodation in a truly unique property. The larger, principal section of the home boasts a Minstrels' Gallery, overlooking a capacious double-height sitting room, dining room and kitchen, with a stylised vaulted ceiling. Three of the four double bedrooms currently on offer are to be found on the mezzanine floor, alongside a "Jack & Jill" en suite, with the main family bathroom located on the ground floor. The layout of the roundal/ "Kiln" area currently comprises an office to the ground floor and fourth double bedroom to the first floor. The possibilities for this property, both inside and out, are countless, including the addition of several bedrooms upstairs. This home really is a must-see!
